Our Mission

The Fellowship promotes Sub-Saharan African culinary heritage as a means to foster community, celebrate diversity, and support sustainable development within the Rotary network. We believe in the power of food to connect people and inspire action.

Flavours

Showcasing the culinary richness of Sub-Saharan Africa

Connections

Taking action to support communities through generosity and local initiatives
