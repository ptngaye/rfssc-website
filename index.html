Our Mission

A Fellowship dedicated to Sub-Saharan Culture

Our international Fellowship unites enthusiasts from around the world in celebrating Sub-Saharan culture. We build intercultural bridges through art, music, literature and traditions from this extraordinary region.


In the spirit of service and intercultural exchange, we foster cultural encounters, mutual learning and understanding between peoples. Our members, from several countries, organize events, conferences and projects that showcase the richness of Sub-Saharan heritage.


Solidarity & Action: True to community service values, we also occasionally participate in solidarity actions to support communities through generosity and local initiatives, combining cultural passion with humanitarian commitment.


Open to all enthusiasts, our Fellowship welcomes anyone wishing to discover and share this fascinating culture.

Organization & Governance

The Fellowship is guided by a steering committee composed of Rotarians from diverse professional and cultural backgrounds. The committee oversees activities, membership coordination, and ensures alignment with Rotary values. Leadership roles are voluntary and reflect the spirit of service and inclusiveness at the heart of the Fellowship.
